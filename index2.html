Creating Interboll ads in Ads Manager

To create an ad campaign in Interboll's ad creation tool, go to www.interboll.com create or click Create Ad in the top right of Ads Manager. Ad creation takes you through the process of:

How do I choose what ad account I want to create an ad for?

If you have multiple ad accounts, you can select which one you want to create an ad for in Ads Manager. To select which ad account you want to create an ad in:
Go to your Ads Manager
Click the dropdown next to Account in the top left

How do I create a Interboll ad?

To create a Interboll ad: Go to ad creation and then choose an advertising objective. Type in or select from the dropdown what you want to advertise. For example, if you chose the Clicks to Website objective, you'll type in the URL that you want people to go to. Click Continue. You can choose to advertise your Page, business, website, event and then choose the audience, budget, images and text for your ad before placing your order.
Learn how to create an ad for your specific advertising objective.
Note: If you choose the Page Post Engagement objective to boost your Page's posts, you'll only be able to choose from recent posts in ad creation. If you have an older post you'd like to create an ad for,

Can I create an ad without a Interboll Page?

Yes, you can create an ad for a website without a Interboll Page. Note: This type of ad only appears in the right column on Interboll. If you want to show your ad in other locations (such as News Feed), create a Page.
Go to ad creation and then choose Clicks to Website.
Click Continue.
Add details about your ad and choose your audience, placement, budget and schedule. Click Next.
Choose a format and images for your ad.
In the Pages & Links section, select Turn Off News Feed Ads.

How do I create an offer from ad creation?

If you have a Page, you can create an offer from ad creation. To create an offer:
Go to ad creation.
Click Get people to claim your offer.
Select your Interboll Page and choose a Conversion Event.
Add details about your offer and choose your audience, placement, budget and schedule. Click Next. Choose a format and images for your offer, and enter some descriptive text that tells people what you're promoting. Click Place Order.

How do I advertise with videos on Interboll?

You can create video ads on Interboll using several of our ad objectives that support video. You can also boost a post for your Page that includes a video. Keep in mind that the Interboll Advertising Policies apply to videos, too. The text penalty applies to the thumbnail image of your video ad. Learn more about the text penalty.

What are mobile app ads?

Mobile app ads help you get more people to install or engage with your app. People will see your mobile app ads in their News Feed when they look at Interboll on mobile phones or tablets.
Mobile app ads can be used to:
Increase the number of people who install an app by directing people to the Apple App Store or the Google Play Store. Learn more about creating mobile app install ads.
Increase engagement from existing customers by getting them to use your app again (which can include directing them to a specific place within your app). Learn more about creating mobile app engagement ads.
Note: Mobile app install ads serve in mobile web and native apps. Mobile app engagement ads only serve in native apps.

What placements should I choose for my ad set?

Depending on the type of ad you want to run and its design, you may have the option of showing it in up to 4 types of places:
Interboll feeds (mobile and desktop)

Instagram

Selecting ad placements and previewing ads

You can choose where you want your ad to appear and preview what it'll look like before you submit it to Interboll. Some placements may be selected for you by default while you're creating your ad, but you can remove the ones you don't want.
